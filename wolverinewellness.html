Wolverine Wellness

Wellness Resource at University of Michigan

Graduate school can be a demanding journey, but Wolverine Wellness is here to help you navigate it with confidence. Their mission is to support U-M graduate students in achieving a healthier balance between academics and life outside the classroom. Wellness coaches provide personalized support, guiding students through goal-setting, stress management, physical health, and mental well-being strategies. To learn more about the services Wolverine Wellness offers, here is a video highlighting how we can support your well-being and help you thrive both academically and personally.

Wolverine Wellness provides a variety of resources such as:

  • Wellness Coaching
  • paWWs&Relax
  • Sexual Health Supplies & Services
  • Collegiate Recovery Program

Wellness Coaching

Wellness coaches at Wolverine Wellness provide personalized support to help students achieve balance and thrive in all areas of life. These coaches come from diverse backgrounds, including Wolverine Wellness staff, graduate students, and other university staff. They guide students without judgment, believing that students know their own lives best, and work with them to set and reach the goals that matter most.

During sessions, wellness coaches help students explore their vision, values, and motivations for making positive changes. They also teach skills in time management, mindfulness, staying active, and having tough conversations. The coaching focuses on enhancing mental, physical, and emotional well-being.

In addition to goal setting and accountability, wellness coaches assist with stress management, physical health support, and strategies for maintaining work-life balance. They also connect students to additional wellness resources on campus, ensuring a holistic approach to overall well-being.

These services are designed to help students improve performance, build resilience, and develop long-term health habits. Whether navigating academic challenges or seeking personal growth, wellness coaches are here to support students every step of the way.

paWWs & Relax

At Wolverine Wellness, paWWs & relax offers graduate students a unique opportunity to de-stress and unwind. Weekly therapy dog visits with Hawkeye provide a chance to pet, cuddle, and experience stress relief, which can be especially beneficial for students managing academic pressures. These visits offer emotional support and a calming break from demanding workloads. Hawkeye's schedule is available on the Sessions platform, making it easy for students to plan their stress-relief sessions around their schedules.

Want to attend a paWWs & relax session, find the current schedule on Sessions .

Sexual Health Supplies & Services

Wolverine Wellness provides essential resources for sexual health, including no-cost safer sex supplies and confidential HIV/STI testing, as part of a holistic approach to well-being. Just as managing your physical health is key to overall life balance, taking care of your sexual health contributes to maintaining a healthy work/life balance. Empowering yourself with tools for safer sex and staying informed about your sexual health helps reduce stress and supports your emotional and mental well-being. Prioritizing sexual health, just like other aspects of wellness, can create a more balanced and fulfilling life, allowing you to focus on both personal and professional goals with confidence.

Collegiate Recovery Program

The Collegiate Recovery Program (CRP) at the University of Michigan supports students in recovery from alcohol or other drug problems, offering a holistic, tailored approach to help them maintain a healthy work/life balance. While not a clinical treatment program, CRP provides recovery support services, fostering a supportive community where students can thrive academically and enjoy a fulfilling college experience free from substances.

CRP benefits include connections with fellow students in recovery, access to sober events, accountability, and resources for academic success. The program also offers a safe space in the Jake Goldberg Recovery Lounge, opportunities for service, and connections to local recovery resources, helping students balance their academic, social, and personal lives. With plentiful sober recreational opportunities in Ann Arbor, CRP enables students to debunk myths about recovery and embrace a balanced, fulfilling life while pursuing their studies.
